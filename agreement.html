3. My/Our Code of Conduct Commitment

By signing this agreement, I/we commit to abiding by the following code of conduct:

Respect and Inclusivity

I/We will:

  • Treat all attendees with respect, kindness, and dignity
  • Respect personal boundaries and comfort levels
  • Be inclusive of all participants regardless of background, identity, or ability
  • Refrain from harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind

Communication

I/We will:

  • Communicate openly and honestly with the group
  • Address conflicts directly and respectfully
  • Notify organizers immediately of any concerns or issues
  • Respect quiet hours and shared spaces

Shared Spaces

I/We will:

  • Keep shared accommodations clean and tidy
  • Respect others' personal belongings
  • Follow house rules of any rental properties
  • Be mindful of noise levels, especially during late hours

Alcohol and Substances

I/We will:

  • Follow all local laws regarding alcohol and substances
  • Drink responsibly if choosing to consume alcohol
  • Never pressure others to drink or participate in activities they're uncomfortable with
  • Not use illegal drugs

Safety

I/We will:

  • Notify the group if leaving for extended periods
  • Use the buddy system when exploring unfamiliar areas
  • Follow all local laws and regulations
  • Report any safety concerns to organizers immediately

Shared Expenses

I/We will:

  • Pay agreed-upon contributions on time
  • Contribute fairly to group meals and activities
  • Communicate about additional shared expenses

Consequences

I/We understand that violation of this code of conduct may result in:

  • Warning from organizers
  • Requirement to leave the meetup without refund
  • Exclusion from future group events
